Internal Operations & Policy Document
Last Updated: November 2025

------------------------------------------------------------
1. Company Overview
------------------------------------------------------------
Acme Technologies is a software development and AI solutions company
specializing in automation systems, intelligent chatbots, and data-driven
enterprise tools. The company was founded in 2018 and currently operates
from Bangalore, India with a workforce of 220 employees.

Key departments include:
- Research & Development (R&D)
- Product Engineering
- Customer Success
- Sales & Marketing
- Cloud Infrastructure
- HR & Administration

------------------------------------------------------------
2. Product Lines
------------------------------------------------------------
Acme offers three primary product categories:

1. Acme Assist (AI-powered support automation)
   - Built using LLM-based conversation engines
   - Supports 24/7 multilingual customer assistance
   - Integrated with CRM systems like Freshdesk and HubSpot

2. Acme Vision (Computer Vision Platform)
   - Used for defect detection, surveillance analytics, and retail insights
   - Supports real-time video stream processing
   - Offers customizable model pipelines

3. Acme Cloud Suite
   - Provides scalable deployment tools for enterprise applications
   - Includes monitoring dashboards, security alerts, and API gateways

3. Employee Policies
------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Working Hours
- Standard working hours: 9:30 AM – 6:30 PM IST
- Employees may opt for flexible timings with manager approval
- Remote work is allowed for up to 3 days per week

3.2 Leave Policy
- Annual Leave: 18 days
- Sick Leave: 12 days
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks
- Paternity Leave: 15 days

3.3 Code of Conduct
- Employees must adhere to Acme’s confidentiality agreement
- Usage of company systems for personal commercial activity is prohibited
- Any form of harassment or discrimination will lead to disciplinary action

------------------------------------------------------------
4. Security Guidelines
------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 Device Security
- All employee laptops must have company-approved antivirus installed
- Devices should auto-lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- External storage devices are restricted unless whitelisted

4.2 Data Handling
- Sensitive data should only be stored on encrypted work drives
- Sharing documents outside the organization requires approval from
  the Security Office
- Employees must use VPN while accessing internal tools remotely

------------------------------------------------------------
5. Customer Onboarding Process
------------------------------------------------------------
Steps followed when onboarding new enterprise customers:

1. Initial Requirements Call  
   - Conducted by Customer Success and Sales teams  
   - Includes understanding customer expectations  

2. Technical Assessment  
   - R&D evaluates feasibility  
   - Engineering shares integration requirements  

3. Deployment  
   - Cloud team prepares environment and deploys services  
   - Customer Success trains end-users  

4. Post-Launch Support  
   - 24/7 support available  
   - Monthly performance report shared with client  

------------------------------------------------------------
6. Incident Response Plan
------------------------------------------------------------
Severity Levels:
- SEV 1: Critical outage (response within 15 minutes)
- SEV 2: Major service disruption (response within 1 hour)
- SEV 3: Minor issue (response within 24 hours)

Escalation Matrix:
- Level 1: Support Engineer
- Level 2: Technical Lead
- Level 3: Engineering Manager
- Level 4: CTO
